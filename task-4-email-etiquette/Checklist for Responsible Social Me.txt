Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use for College Students

DO’s:

1. Think before you post and ensure your content is respectful.
2. Adjust your privacy settings to ensure your personal information is secure.
3. Ensure you verify information before sharing it with others.
4. Use polite language in your comments and messages.
5. Use social media for learning, networking, and skill building.

DON’Ts:

1. Avoid sharing your personal information such as your phone number and password.
2. Avoid engaging in cyberbullying and arguments.
3. Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, and harmful content.
4. Avoid believing and sharing news and rumors without verifying them.
5. Avoid excessive social media use for studying and in class.
